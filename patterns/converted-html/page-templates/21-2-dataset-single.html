Data is for the most recent available year across all categories; this is 2016, except for private finance mobilised via blending (included in international commercial estimates) and private development assistance (included in international private estimates). Data is in US$ billions, 2016.

For explanations on Domestic and Official resources data used refer to ITEP methodology page

Provenance

International Monetary Fund (IMF), World Bank, UN Conference on Trade and Development, OECD DAC and national sources.

Geography

Global: resource flows to developing countries, as defined by ‘the DAC list of ODA recipients’

Variables

Domestic resources (commercial and public), official resources (public, commercial and official). Figures available with disaggregation excluding China.
