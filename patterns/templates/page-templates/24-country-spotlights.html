The spotlights on Kenya and Uganda are interactive online tools primarily created for government agencies and ministries, advocacy and civil society organisations, and international donors operating in these countries.

They make it possible for technical and non-technical audiences alike to easily access and understand information about what is happening in both countries across a wide range of socioeconomic indicators and financing.

From national to county levels, you can map data, see key statistics with ease, and explore detailed comparisons between different areas and indicators. Where data does not exist, this is highlighted in the tool to show where efforts to improve data coverage in each country need to be focussed.
